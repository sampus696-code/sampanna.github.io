Who I Am

I am a child actor, public speaker, and science enthusiast with a passion for learning and creating. My journey began in theatre arts at the age of 5, and since then, I've explored the worlds of cinema, advertisements, and music videos.

Beyond the screen, I am deeply involved in social initiatives like Engineers Without Borders Nepal (Junior Chapter) and academic pursuits, recently topping the National Astronomy Olympiad. I believe in the power of connection, continuous learning, and the magic of reading.
